The Gantt Chart

Different appearances of the gantt chart can be choosen in the menu Options|Gantt Chart if a schedule is present.

Basic Choices

  • Orientation: Choose job oriented or machine oriented display. In a job oriented gantt chart (each job in one seperate row), also release times and due dates are displayed.
  • Special Emphasis: Beside the appearance "Normal" (fixed assignment of up to 23 well distinguished colors) you can choose "Critical Path" (operations on a critical path are red, all others gray) or you can choose some colors for special jobs.
    Critical pathes are only recognized for a C_max objective criterion.

Functions in the Gantt Chart

  • This view (like the sequence graph) can be zoomed. Click on the magnifying glass symbol or activate the zoom mode in the menu Extras. Draw a rectrangle with the mouse to make this the new view. To return to the initial view, deaktivate the zoom mode.
  • Double click on an operation invokes the Sequence Editor to shift this operation.
